Community

Superficial details about how the situation in the community is.

  • General details
    • 6000 inhabitants.
    • 1h trip from Monrovia city centre by motorized vehicle.
    • 7:10 woman and 3:10 men ratios.
    • Normal income in community is less than 750Liberian Dollars or equivalent to 5 US dollars every month per person.
  • Terrain desc
    • Community lives in flood plain and marsh area, with isolated water areas in alluvial plain and terraces.
    • Floodings occur during rainy season. When water overpass the load capacity of the swamp terrain, it flows to Garza community where it does not connect to any river, therefore creating the floods.
    • Run off contaminates unprotected shallow wells and increases the breeding of bacteria, generated from excrement and waste.
  • WASH conditions
    • Commonly accessible sources of drinking water by majority of the residents are unprotected wells.
    • Hand washing is a rare practice among residents.
    • Disposal of human waste is through open defecation. Majority of the adults use “the bush”, whereas stools from children are disposed of by “burying in the yard”.
    • The water points that exist today are in terrible condition and the vast majority are open wells/pits. As for the latrines, if they are not closed, they are collapsed and filled out.
    • The community is in lack of new facilities and a long-term care model.
