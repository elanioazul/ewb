Guidelines

3 pilars of the project

Clean water & sanitation

Sustainable management of water resources and access to safe water and sanitation are essential for unlocking economic growth and productivity, and provide significant leverage for existing investments in health and education.

Colaboration

The support to the Local Water and Sanitation commite coupled with local partnership comes within a series of activities delivered through Local Partner and EWB.

Local empowerment

The project focuses on local capacity building and the rehabilitation of infrastructure to reduce water-related diseases; contribute to a stronger economy by increasing productivity; and, contribute to stability by allowing communities to enjoy the benefits of peace.
